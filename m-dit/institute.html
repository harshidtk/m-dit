About

Touch modern concepts and learn more practices.

M-DIT POLYTECHNIC COLLEGE, Established on the campus at ulliyeri inthe academic year 2015. The Polytechnic College offers diploma programmes of three year duration in four disciplines, All the programmes are approved by AICTE, New Delhi and the State Board of Technical education,Kerala.Efforts are on develop the M-DIT Campus into a full-fledged Technological Campus churning out the best technically qualified and industry ready candidates in all the fields og engineering.

Our
Campus:

M-DIT KOZHIKODE is a venture of M. Dasan Memorial Co-operative institute of Engineering and Information Technology. In a short span of time, the institute has carved a niche in the field of technical education in the state through its advanced courses in engineering and technology and has become one of the most preferred institutions for aspiring students in the state.
